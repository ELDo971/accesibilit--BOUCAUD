About Us

Full Throttle Sundays is your one-stop shop for all things Formula 1! We're a community of racing fanatics dedicated to bringing you the hottest news, insightful analysis, and the electrifying spirit of F1 every single race day (and maybe even some Mondays!). Whether you're a die-hard tifosi (fan) or just discovering the world of high-octane competition, we welcome you with open arms (and maybe a celebratory shoey!).

Join us as we dissect driver strategies, analyze pit stop dramas, and celebrate (or commiserate with) the champions. Get ready to experience the roar of the engines, the strategic battles on the track, and the pure adrenaline rush that is Formula 1. Buckle up, hit the gas, and get ready for a full-throttle ride with Full Throttle Sundays!
